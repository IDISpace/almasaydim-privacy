Privacy Policy — Almasaydım

Last updated: 19 May 2026

This privacy policy explains how the "Almasaydım" mobile application (the "App") handles user data. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Data Collected and Storage

Almasaydım is a personal savings-tracking application. All information you enter into the App — including the amounts you save or spend, categories, notes, goals, your profile name and profile photo — is stored locally on your own device only.

This data is never sent to any server, cannot be accessed by the developer, and is not shared with any third parties. Your data remains entirely under your control.

2. Internet and Server Use

The App does not transmit your personal data over the internet and does not store it on any remote server. All processing of your data happens entirely on your device.

3. Device Permissions

The App may request the following permissions from your device for specific features:

  • Photo / Gallery access: Used solely so you can choose a profile photo. The photo you select is stored only on your device.
  • Notification permission: Used to show you reminders and motivational notifications. Notifications are generated entirely on your device.
  • Storage access: Used so you can create and save backup and report (PDF/Excel) files.

These permissions are optional and only required when you use the related feature. You may choose not to grant them; in that case only the related feature will not work.

4. Data Backup

The App allows you to export your data as a file. This file is saved only to a location you choose (your device or your own cloud account). Where the backup file is saved and who it is shared with is entirely under your control. The developer cannot access these files.

5. Advertising and Analytics

The App does not contain advertisements. The App does not use any analytics or user-tracking tools.

6. Optional Support (Donation) Link

The App may in the future include a donation link for users who wish to voluntarily support the developer. When you tap such a link, a third-party service (for example, a donation platform) opens in your device's browser. Donating is entirely optional and is not required for any feature of the App.

The donation transaction and any related payment information are handled by the relevant third-party service; this process takes place outside the App. The Almasaydım app does not collect, see, or store this payment information. The third-party service's own privacy policy applies.

7. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal data from children. All data is local and consists of information entered by the user themselves.

8. Deletion of Your Data

When you uninstall the App from your device, all local data belonging to the App is also deleted. You can also delete your data from within the App at any time.

9. Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page and the "Last updated" date will be revised.
